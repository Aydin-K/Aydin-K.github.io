About Me

My mother is Australian and my father is Turkish. I was born in Australia so that I could have citizenship there. After living in Australia with my mother’s family for about 6 months, we moved back to Turkey so that we could be with my older brother and father again.

Around the year 2000, I moved to Utah and began my education in the United States.

In 2012 I entered Utah State University in an attempt to pursue a degree in medicine. Turns out, I hated the classes I would need to take. To that end, I changed my major to Communication.

After several years of stress, moving cities, changing schools, and even getting married, I finally graduated with a Communication Studies B.A. from Virginia Tech.

All of that said, I am still a person and not a fact sheet. I love videogames and making people laugh. I enjoy spending time with my wife, our silly cats, and my friends. Past that, I like taking walks, watching funny movies, a wide variety of anime, and generally enjoying life.

If you have read this far, I thank you. It means a lot to me that you took the time to learn a bit more about me. Hopefully something on this page, or one of the links, has made you smile and brightened your day.
